Description: Changepay is a customizable commerce platform that aims to change the way business and communication conducted in large residential campuses & close-knit communities by empowering local merchants & enterprises to provide delightful service, while ensuring a convenient and seamless experience for the student/consumer. We are leveraging our Smartbox technology to solve problems of residential students and help campus to achieve effective social distancing once the students are back in the hostel as per government recommendation.
